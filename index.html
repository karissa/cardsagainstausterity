Where does the money go?

Donations support coordinated direct action.

The costs associated with public protest are a burden for those already struggling. Your support will go toward travel, food, bail and lodging to remove financial barriers to activism for people buried in debt. In addition, the more money we have, the more organizers, strategists, technologists, lawyers, artists, and administrative staff we can pay for their hard and ongoing work.

All donations are tax deductible.
